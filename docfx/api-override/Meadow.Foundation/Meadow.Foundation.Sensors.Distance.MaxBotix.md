### Wiring Example

To wire a MaxBotix to your Meadow board, connect the following:

| MaxBotix  | Meadow Pin  |
|---------|-------------|
| GND     | GND         |
| SCL     | D08 (SCL)   |
| SDA     | D07 (SDA)   |
| VCC     | 3V3         |
