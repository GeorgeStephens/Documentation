### Wiring Example

To wire a Ads1115 to your Meadow board, connect the following:

| Ads1115  | Meadow Pin    |
|---------|---------------|
| GND     | GND           |
| VCC     | 3V3           |
| SCL     | D08 (SCL Pin) |
| SDA     | D07 (SDA Pin) |
